Choose-your-own-adventure stories, web comics & interactive books

Looking for choose your own adventure online? Pick Your Path Stories (PYPStories) lets you read branching narratives the way classic CYOA books worked — pick a choice, jump to a new page, and shape the ending — with art and layout built for the web.

Creators can publish interactive comics, illustrated paths inspired by webtoons, and digital books where readers decide what happens next. Upload pages, connect choices, set a free sample or a price, and share a link with anyone.

Whether you love interactive fiction, web comics, or story games in book form, start on the home page catalog, open a story, and pick your path.
